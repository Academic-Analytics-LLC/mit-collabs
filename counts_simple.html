MIT Collaboration — Simple Counts

One row per entity. Every number is a distinct-works total — no partner breakdown, no person-pairs. Within=has an internal co-author at this same entity · Across=has an MIT co-author elsewhere · Inter=has an external co-author · Intra=within∪across · All=any qualifying collaboration. These are honest totals; they will not match the partner-summed anchored numbers on the Counts (Partners) page (a work spanning 2 partner depts counts once per partner there, once total here — e.g. Physics across = 231 there vs 192 here). MIT institution totals below are unit-type independent.
