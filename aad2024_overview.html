AAD2024 Snapshot

About this dataset

The authoritative picture of current MIT faculty collaborations, drawn from the research works in the AAD2024 data cycle — roughly 1,270 scholars and 16,747 works.

Counting conventions (Within/Across/Inter, distinct works vs. partner-summed) are documented in the Key.

This is a point-in-time snapshot, not a longitudinal record. For multi-year external engagement, see International & Industry.
